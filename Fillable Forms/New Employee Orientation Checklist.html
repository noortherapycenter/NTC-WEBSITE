Human Resources · Onboarding

New Employee Orientation Checklist

Initial and date each item as it is completed. File the finished checklist in the personnel record alongside the Employee File Checklist.

1

Employee

2

Before the First Day

ItemDateInitials
Signed offer letter received
DHS NETStudy background study initiated
New-hire packet completed (W-4, I-9, direct deposit, consents)
Assurance statement (DHS-7120) signed with credentials attached
3

First Day

ItemDateInitials
Tour of the center; introductions to the team
Employee Handbook provided; acknowledgement signed
HIPAA & confidentiality training; agreement signed
Emergency procedures, exits, and incident reporting reviewed
Mandated reporting duties reviewed (minors & vulnerable adults)
Schedule, timekeeping, and call-out procedure explained
4

First Week

ItemDateInitials
EHR access set up; documentation standards reviewed
Required trainings assigned (per Required Staff Training policy)
Shadow sessions with experienced provider
Supervision schedule set with QSP
Client assignments reviewed, incl. behavior plans & medical needs
5

First 30 Days

ItemDateInitials
Required trainings completed and documented
Competency check / observation by QSP
30-day check-in meeting with supervisor
6

Sign-Off

All orientation items above have been completed and the employee has had the opportunity to ask questions.
