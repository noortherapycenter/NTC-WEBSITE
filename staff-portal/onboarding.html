Pick the provider level. Each page lists the qualification requirements and the full new-hire packet.

QSP QSP / BCBAQualified Supervising Professional / Board Certified Behavior Analyst → L1 Level I ProviderAdvanced provider — intervention, observation & direction, family training → L2 Level II ProviderBehavior technician — direct intervention under QSP direction → L3 Level III ProviderBehavior technician — direct intervention under QSP direction →
Every new hire, regardless of level, completes the same 10-item mandatory packet: employment application, level-specific offer letter, DHS-4138, level-specific DHS-7120 assurance statement, W-4, I-9, direct deposit, background check consent, handbook acknowledgement, and the confidentiality / HIPAA agreement. Each level page lists it in order with links.
